Blackjack Rules

Blackjack is a popular card game played in casinos around the world. The objective of the game is to have a hand value as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it. Here are the basic rules:

  • Each player is dealt two cards, and the dealer also receives two cards (one face up and one face down).
  • Players can choose to "hit" (take another card) or "stand" (keep their current hand).
  • If a player's hand exceeds 21, they "bust" and lose the game.
  • The dealer must hit until their hand value is 17 or higher.
  • If the dealer busts, all remaining players win.
  • If neither the player nor the dealer busts, the hand with the higher value wins.
  • Aces can be worth 1 or 11, face cards are worth 10, and all other cards are worth their face value.

Good luck and have fun playing Blackjack!
