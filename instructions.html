How To Play

Blackjack

  • Get 2 cards; dealer gets 2
  • Card values: number, face = 10, Ace = 1 or 11
  • Hit to take a card, Stand to keep your hand
  • Don't go over 21
  • Dealer hits until 17+
  • Closest to 21 wins; tie = push

Baccarat

  • Bet on Player, Banker, or Tie
  • Two hands are dealt (Player & Banker)
  • Card values: numbers = number, 10/face = 0, Ace = 1
  • Add cards; keep last digit only
  • Sometimes a third card is drawn
  • Hand closest to 9 wins
